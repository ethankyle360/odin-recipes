Avocado Toast Recipe

This recipe is avocado and a toast of bread and spreading the avocado on the toasted bread.

Ingredients

  • avocado
  • toast
  • toaster

Steps

  1. Get your avocado and wash it.
  2. Use a spoon and get the avocado.
  3. Toast your bread.
  4. When your done toasting, spread the avocado on the bread.
